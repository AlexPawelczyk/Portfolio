About Me

My name is Alex Pawelczyk and I am a current graduate student at the University of Waterloo's David R. Cheriton School of Computer Science. My passion for computer science began in high school and has continued to grow throughout my university career. Hockey has also played a major role in my life, and I've been fortunate to play the game I love for over 20 years. I was fortunate to receive athletic scholarships at Dalhousie University and the University of Waterloo to play on the varsity hockey teams and pursue my education. I learned a lot of valuable lessons as a student-athlete, and I am forever thankful for the chance I was given to pursue my passions of computer science and hockey.
